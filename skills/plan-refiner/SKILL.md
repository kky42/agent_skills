---
name: plan-refiner
description: Refine a plan or strategy before execution. Use when the user wants a plan, approach, roadmap, or next step stress-tested, loophole-checked, or made more robust, whether it came from the user or the agent.
---

Stress-test the current plan before execution. Restate it briefly, find the material loopholes, assumptions, edge cases, hidden dependencies, and failure modes, then propose concrete fixes and a revised plan.

Run the critique once more against the revised plan. Stop when no known high-impact gaps remain, or when more confidence requires missing facts, tests, or user decisions.

Do not claim 100% certainty. State the remaining uncertainty and the next verification step.
